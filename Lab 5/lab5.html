About Wave to Earth

Wave to Earth is a rising South Korean indie band known for their distinctive sound that blends elements of indie rock, jazz, and dream pop. Formed in 2019, the band consists of three members: Daniel Kim (vocals/guitar), John Lee (drums), and Sunny Park (bass). With their emotionally-driven lyrics, soothing instrumentals, and atmospheric production, Wave to Earth has carved out a niche in the indie music scene both in Korea and internationally.

Their debut EP, "Wave 0.01," introduced their minimalist yet emotionally rich style, which gained attention from both local and international listeners. Since then, they have released several tracks and albums, such as "Seasons" and "Peach Eyes," which have further solidified their reputation as one of the most promising indie bands in Korea.
